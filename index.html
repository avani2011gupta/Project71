Campus Life

School Library

Every school has a library and like every school my school too, has a big and spacious library. It is a huge classroom filled with bookshelves, racks, tables and chairs and has enough room for students in 3 sections (with 40 students in each section) to read. It has enormous windows to let enough light in. It is the most quiet place in the school.



Big Field

It is a huge field in which the students can do all sports and games related activities. It has seperate sections for badminton, basketball, yoga, cricket, hokey, horse riding, chess, etc. The annual day and the sports day is also held over here.



School Auditorium

A school auditorium is a special place for the special events that are conducted in school. It has a hallowed place and a stage. It has a mike and speaker for audio effects. It has lots of rows and columns of seats for all students and teachers and also special cushioned chairs for special guests. It has a good backdrop on the stage and small stairs on both sides of the stage.

School Achievements

Best School Award
Amity International School, Noida, won the “Best School Award”.

Amity International School, Noida, won the “Best School Award” during the 3rd National Water Awards 2020 contest, launched by the Department of Water Resources, River Development & Ganga Rejuvenation, Government of India. The school secured the Third Position at the National Level and the First Position at the State Level. The results were declared in the presence of Shri Gajendra Singh Shekhawat, Minister of Jal Shakti, Department of Water Affairs, River Development and Ganga Rejuvenation.

CBSE Central Zone Chess Tournament
CBSE Central Zone Chess Tournament

The Under-11 Chess team comprising Parnika Agarwal (Class IV), Anvi Goel (Class VI), Shreya Chakrabarty (Class V) and Mansha Garg (Class V) performed exceptionally well and bagged the bronze medal at the Chess Tournament organised by CBSE from October 4- 7, 2017, held at Rukmini Devi Public School.

Science Fair
Northern India Science Fair

February 1-2, 2019 School participated and won various prizes at Northern India Science Fair (city level) held at New Delhi. Shashwat Shrivastava of Class VIII received a special prize in the individual category and was awarded a trophy, a certificate and cash prize of Rs.500/- while Arnav Praneet and Debodhwani Mishra of Class IX came second in team category and received a trophy, a certificate and cash prize of Rs. 1500/-.
